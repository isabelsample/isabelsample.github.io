About Joe

Joe Abrams is a musician ~ A songwriter, vocalist, performing & recording artist, multi-instrumentalist, collaborator, teacher and music publisher.

He has a heavily improvisational approach to performing his songs and the rare cover song. He has recorded several albums in which he wrote and played all of the instruments.

Joe has also been in groups with the musical talents of Brad Houser (Edie Brickell and the New Bohemians), Ben Shepherd (Soundgarden) and has collaborated with Andy Wood (Mother Love Bone) and Stone Gossard (MLB & Pearl Jam).
